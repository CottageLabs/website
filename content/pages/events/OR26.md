We are [sponsoring](https://or2026.openrepositories.org/sponsors/supporters-2026/) the Open Repositories Conference 2026, a virtual event bringing the community together to explore open infrastructure, repositories, and scholarly systems.

## Our presentations

* **Public Health Data in Context: Publishing the RKI research graph**

    _Richard Jones, (Date & Time TBC)_

    Presenting with the Robert Koch Institute, we explore how InvenioRDM supports a public health research graph, linking complex data from projects to datasets.

* **Ask a robot: adding AI search to a repository**

    _Steve Eardley, (Date & Time TBC)_

    We take a practical look at AI-powered search in repositories, moving beyond keywords to more meaningful discovery.

* **Designing repository interfaces for cognitive accessibility**

    _Aga Domańska, (Date & Time TBC)_

    Explore how better UX can reduce cognitive load and make repository systems easier to use in practice.
