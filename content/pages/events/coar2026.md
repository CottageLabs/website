We were pleased to be invited to update the COAR community on our work to support the development of the COAR Notify protocol, and to share our experience of building tools to support its adoption.  We have been working with the COAR Notify team, the InvenioRDM team, as well as Peer-Community-In and Episciences to enable InvenioRDM and Zenodo to support the protocol.

**When?** 1 - 3 June 2026

**Where?** Braga, Portugal and streamed worldwide
