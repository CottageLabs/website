# Privacy Policy

Cottage Labs is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your personal information when you visit our website.

## Information We Collect

If you have opted-in to analytics cookies, we may collect information about your visit to our website, including but not limited to:

- Your IP address
- Browser type and version
- Time and date of your visit
- Pages you visit on our website
- Referring website (if applicable)
- Device information (e.g., operating system, screen resolution)

We will *not* collect personally identifiable information such as your name, email address, or any other information that can be used to identify you unless you voluntarily provide it to us through contact forms or other means.

## How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to analyse website traffic and user behavior to improve our website and services.  The aggregated and anonymised data will be stored securely within an external analytics provider.

We will not share your information with third parties for marketing or any other purposes.

## Opting Out of Analytics Cookies

You will be prompted to opt-in to analytics cookies when you first visit our website. If you choose to opt-out, we will not collect any information about your visit. You can also manage your cookie preferences through your browser settings.
