ABOUT US

Cottage Labs is a partnership of software and data experts who can help you understand and work with your data more effectively.

We excel in building Open Access infrastructure, data search and visualisations and data repositories, and can build tailored solutions for your needs, from software to processes. Our expertise extends across your entire project lifecycle; we can help you from your initial idea, through the capture and analysis of your requirements, to implementation, long-term maintenance and hosting, providing effective project management along the way.

Whatever your data needs are, from capturing at source, to tools for management, analysis and insights, right through to making your data available and useful for others, Cottage Labs can help you.
